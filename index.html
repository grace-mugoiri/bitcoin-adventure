1. What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is digital money that works without a bank. It's fast, secure, and worldwide.

2. Who Created Bitcoin?

It was created by someone (or a group) named Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009.

3. What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is like a public notebook where every Bitcoin transaction is recorded forever.

4. Is Bitcoin Safe?

Yes! It uses strong cryptography and runs on a decentralized network of computers.

5. Can I Use Bitcoin?

Of course! You can send, receive, and spend Bitcoin using a Bitcoin wallet.
